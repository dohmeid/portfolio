About Me

Who is Doha?

Greetings! I'm Doha Hmeid, a computer systems engineering graduate from Birzeit University, where I graduated with honors. Currently in my final semester, I'm diligently completing my remaining academic hours to officially receive my degree.

In the previous semester, I successfully concluded my graduation project, crafting an Android mobile application tailored to enhance the experience of visitors navigating through the intricate landscape of Birzeit University. This innovative app seamlessly integrates dynamic mapping functionalities, virtual tours, and real-time guidance, ensuring users have a comprehensive and secure campus exploration.

My Story!

My journey into web development started when I stumbled upon a web development course ad while scrolling through Facebook in 2021. I decided to give it a shot and instantly fell in love with the process of translating code into tangible websites.

Subsequently, when the time for my university's summer internship arrived, I decided to pursue training in front-end development. I enrolled in an intensive 2-month training program at EXALT Technologies, where I delved into HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the React framework. It was exhilarating to see my skills evolve and solidify. This experience marked my first real training in the field, and I enjoyed it so much that I resolved to pursue a career in front-end development.

Realizing the need for further training to delve into intricacies, best practices, and advanced topics in front-end development to enhance my readiness for the competitive job market, I enrolled in Foothill Technology Solutions' training program. Currently, I am expanding my skills and gaining more hands-on experience in the field.

To me, front-end development is more than just coding; it's an ever-evolving journey that demands creativity, adaptability, and a deep understanding of browser dynamics. I'm driven by the thrill of mastering this complex craft and shaping meaningful digital experiences.

Looking towards the future fills me with excitement and anticipation. Each day brings new opportunities for learning, growth, and innovation. I am eager to embrace whatever surprises lie ahead, prepared to leave my mark in the ever-evolving tech universe.
